For the grown-up 👋 This playground follows the silent period / comprehensible-input approach: meaning comes from pictures, sound and repetition — never from grammar rules or translation. Let her explore in any order, repeat favourites, and respond with her body or a single word whenever she is ready. Best opened in Chrome, Edge or Safari with sound on. 🔊
